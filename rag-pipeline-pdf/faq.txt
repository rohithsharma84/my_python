[Product Ordering]
Q: Can I change or cancel my order after it has been placed?
A: Orders are processed quickly to ensure fast delivery. You can cancel or modify your order within 30 minutes of placement by visiting your Order History page or contacting support. After 30 minutes, the order cannot be changed, but you can return the items once received.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and our store electronic gift cards.

Q: Why was my credit card charged twice for my order?
A: When you place an order, a temporary authorization hold is placed on your card to reserve funds. This is not an actual charge. The true charge occurs when your order ships, at which point the authorization hold will automatically drop off within 3-5 business days.

Q: Do you offer bulk or corporate discounts?
A: Yes, we offer volume-based pricing for corporate orders, schools, and organizations purchasing more than 20 units of a single item. Please contact our corporate sales team via email at corporate@nexusretail.com for a custom quote.

Q: How do I know if an item is backordered, and when will it arrive?
A: Backordered items are clearly marked on the product page with an estimated shipping date. If you purchase a backordered item, we will ship the rest of your order immediately and send the backordered item as soon as it arrives at no extra shipping cost.

[Delivery & Shipping]
Q: How can I track the shipping status of my package?
A: Once your order ships, we will send a confirmation email containing your tracking number and a direct link to the carrier's portal. You can also view real-time tracking updates in your account under the "My Orders" tab.

Q: What are your standard shipping rates and delivery timelines?
A: We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) for a flat rate of $5.99, or free for orders over $50. Expedited shipping (1-2 business days) is available for $14.99. Deliveries to Alaska, Hawaii, and P.O. Boxes may take an additional 2-3 business days.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Currently, we only ship to addresses within the United States, Canada, and U.S. Territories. We plan to expand to international shipping in Europe and Australia by late 2026.

Q: My tracking link says "Delivered," but I cannot find my package. What should I do?
A: Carriers sometimes mark packages as delivered up to 24 hours before actual arrival. Please check around your property, with neighbors, or your building's front desk. If it still hasn't arrived after 24 hours, contact us within 7 days so we can open an investigation.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after my order has shipped?
A: Once a package has left our fulfillment center, we cannot redirect it to a new address. We recommend contacting the carrier (UPS or FedEx) directly with your tracking number to request a package hold or reroute if they support it.

[Returns & Refunds]
Q: What is your return policy, and how long do I have to return an item?
A: We accept returns of unworn, unwashed, or unused items in their original packaging within 30 days of the delivery date. Electronics and software must be unopened to qualify for a full refund.

Q: How do I initiate a return and get a shipping label?
A: To start a return, log into your account, go to "My Orders," select the order containing the item, and click "Initiate Return." You will be able to print a pre-paid USPS shipping label. A return shipping fee of $4.50 will be deducted from your final refund.

Q: When will I receive my refund after returning an item?
A: Once our warehouse receives and inspects your returned package, we process your refund within 3-5 business days. It may take an additional 2-10 business days for your banking institution to post the credit to your original payment method.

Q: Can I exchange an item for a different size or color instead of getting a refund?
A: Yes. When initiating your return online, select the "Exchange" option instead of "Refund." You can pick your new size or color immediately, and we will ship the new item out free of charge as soon as your return package is scanned by the post office.

Q: What should I do if my item arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect?
A: We are so sorry for the inconvenience. Please send an email to support@nexusretail.com within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and clear photos of the damaged or incorrect item. We will ship out a replacement immediately and provide a waiver for the return shipping.

[Price Matching]
Q: Do you offer price matching if I find an item cheaper elsewhere?
A: Yes! We match the current, advertised price of identical, in-stock items from major authorized online retailers (including Amazon, Walmart, and Target). The item must match exactly in brand, model, size, and color.

Q: How do I request a price match before making a purchase?
A: Before placing your order, open a live chat with our customer support team or call us at 1-800-555-0199. Provide the active URL link to the competitor's product page showing the lower price. Once verified, our agent will apply a one-time discount code to your cart.

Q: Can I get a price adjustment if an item goes on sale after I buy it?
A: We offer a 14-day price protection window. If an item you purchased on our website drops in price or goes on sale within 14 days of your purchase date, contact us to receive a store credit or refund for the difference.

Q: Are there any exclusions to your price match policy?
A: Yes. We do not match clearance, closeout, liquidation sales, open-box items, doorbusters, holiday promotional events (such as Black Friday or Cyber Monday), or prices from third-party marketplace sellers on Amazon or eBay.

Q: Can I combine a price match with another promotional coupon or discount code?
A: No, price matches cannot be combined with any other coupon codes, rewards points, sign-up discounts, or sitewide promotional offers. The price match replaces all other discounts for that specific item.
